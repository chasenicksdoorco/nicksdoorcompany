Our Services

Complete commercial door and dock equipment solutions — installation, repair, and maintenance

Dock Levelers

Hydraulic, mechanical, and air-powered dock levelers for safe, efficient loading.

Get a Quote
We install and service all types of dock levelers including hydraulic, mechanical, and air-powered models. Our levelers ensure smooth transitions between truck beds and loading docks, improving safety and efficiency. We carry top brands and offer custom solutions for any dock configuration.

Truck Restraints

Vehicle restraint systems to keep trucks secure during loading and unloading.

Get a Quote
Our truck restraint systems prevent premature truck departure during loading, protecting your workers and cargo. We install wheel-based and hook-based restraints with integrated communication lights to signal safe loading status.

High-Speed Doors

High-performance rapid roll doors for high-traffic commercial areas.

Get a Quote
High-speed doors minimize air exchange, reduce energy costs, and improve traffic flow. Ideal for warehouses, cold storage, and manufacturing facilities with high forklift traffic. We install and service all major brands.

Sectional Doors

Insulated and non-insulated commercial sectional overhead doors.

Get a Quote
From standard commercial sectional doors to heavy-duty insulated models, we provide doors that fit your facility's needs. Available in multiple gauges, insulation values, and configurations with window and pass-door options.

Rolling Steel Doors

Heavy-duty roll-up steel doors for maximum security and durability.

Get a Quote
Rolling steel doors are the go-to solution for security, durability, and space savings. We install service doors, counter shutters, and heavy-duty rolling doors built to withstand demanding commercial and industrial environments.

Fire Doors

Fire-rated doors meeting all code requirements for your facility.

Get a Quote
Fire-rated rolling steel doors and sectional doors that meet UL and local fire code requirements. We handle installation, inspection, and annual drop-testing to keep your facility compliant and safe.

Maintenance Contracts

Preventive maintenance programs to extend equipment life and avoid downtime.

Get a Quote
Our preventive maintenance contracts keep your doors and dock equipment running reliably. Regular inspections catch problems before they become expensive emergencies. Customizable plans for any size facility.

Common questions about commercial doors and dock equipment

How fast can you provide a quote?

We provide detailed quotes within 24 hours for most projects. For emergency situations, we can often quote on the spot. Contact us and we will get back to you fast.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Taylorsville and provide service and repair across the Wasatch Front from Logan and Brigham City down to Provo. For new installations, we also cover St. George, Idaho, and Wyoming.

Do you offer emergency repair service?

Yes! We offer 24/7 emergency repair for all commercial doors and dock equipment. Call us anytime at (801) 864-6207 and we will be there as fast as possible.

What brands do you install and service?

We are authorized dealers for Poweramp (Systems LLC), Wayne Dalton, Amarr, Clopay, Hormann, Dynaco, TKO, and Albany. We can also service most other major brands.

Who does the installations?

The owners — Nick, Chase, and Cheyenne — personally handle every installation. With 30+ years of experience, you get expert-level quality on every job. No subcontractors, no middlemen.

Do you offer maintenance contracts?

Yes. Our preventive maintenance programs include regular inspections, lubrication, adjustments, and priority emergency service. Maintenance contracts help avoid costly breakdowns and extend equipment life.
